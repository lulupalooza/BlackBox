GPIO_pwr_outis the GPIO pin used for PWM output signal to resistor/heater inside black box.

This task is used to determine the output signal to the resistor/heater inside the black box. It will adjust the PWM signal used to drive the GPIO connected to the heater system, and therefore the temp of the black box, according to input from PID controller module.
